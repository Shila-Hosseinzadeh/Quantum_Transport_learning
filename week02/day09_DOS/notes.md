# Day 09 - Density of States (DOS)

## Concept
Density of States (DOS) describes how many quantum states are available at a given energy.

## Key points
- DOS tells us how many electronic states exist at each energy.
- If DOS(E_F) > 0, electrons can occupy states near the Fermi level and conduction is possible.
- If DOS(E_F) = 0, the material behaves as a semiconductor or insulator.
- Metals have finite DOS at the Fermi level.

## Relation to MTJ
In Fe/MgO/Fe magnetic tunnel junctions, the DOS near the Fermi level strongly influences the tunneling current and transport properties.
